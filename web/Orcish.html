Orcish words are gathered off the Internet, SMAUG1.8, made up myself, etc. They originate or are inspired by JRR Tolkien's Orcish; this file has had many incarnations and was ported from Java. Random words are super-useful in testing and character generation; http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Languages_constructed_by_J._R._R._Tolkien. Provides one function, Orcish.

Orcish

void Orcish (char *const name, const size_t name_size)

Takes name, a string, and replaces it, to a maximum of name_size characters, with a rand Orcish name. You must have space for (at least) name_size (byte) characters.

parameter: name
Filled with a random word in psudo-Orcish.
parameter: name_size
sizeof(name); suggest 16, which would be enough for 2 syllables and a suffix.
